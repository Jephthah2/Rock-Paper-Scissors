# Rock Paper Scissors
 A simple **rock, paper, scissor** game.
 Players can choose between rock, paper and scissors and the computer randomly picks one.

 ## How to Play
 1. Choose **Rock**, **Paper**, or **Scissors**.
 2. The computer will also make its move.
 3. The winner is decided based on the rules:
    * Rock beats Scissors
    * Scissors beats Paper
    * Paper beats Rock
